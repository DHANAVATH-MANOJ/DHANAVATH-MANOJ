IN LOVING MEMORY OF SUSHANTH

1996-2020

DETAILED INFORMATION

EARLY LIFE:

- Sushant Singh Rajput was born on January 21, 1986, in Patna, Bihar, India. - He pursued Mechanical Engineering at Delhi Technological University but dropped out to follow his passion for acting.

TELEVISION CAREER:

- Sushant gained widespread recognition for his role as Manav in the TV series "Pavitra Rishta" (2009-2011), which became immensely popular.

BOLLY WOOD DEBUE AND SUCESS:

- He made his Bollywood debut with "Kai Po Che!" in 2013, receiving critical acclaim for his performance. - Notable films include "Shuddh Desi Romance," "Detective Byomkesh Bakshy!," and "PK."

BREAK THROUGH BIOPIC:

- Sushant's portrayal of cricketer MS Dhoni in "MS Dhoni: The Untold Story" (2016) was a turning point in his career.

PASSING AND LEGACY:

- Tragically, Sushant Singh Rajput passed away on June 14, 2020, at the age of 34. - His untimely death sparked conversations about mental health and nepotism in the film industry.

Sushant left a lasting impact on Indian cinema, remembered for his talent, versatility, and the discussions he ignited within the industry.
